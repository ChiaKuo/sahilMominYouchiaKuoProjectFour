Harry Potter Adventures

Welcome! Harry Potter Adventures is a choose your own adventure game based on the world of Harry Potter. Choose your name, get sorted into one of the four houses, and embark on one of many scenarios to rid the world evil.

"You might belong in Gryffindor, where dwell the brave at heart, their daring, nerve and chivalry set Gryffindors apart."

Notable Students: Harry Potter, Albus Dumbledore, Hermione Granger, Ron Weasley, Minerva McGonagall

Values: Chivalry, Bravery, Daring, Courage

"Or perhaps in Slytherin, you'll make your real friends, those cunning folk use any means, to achieve their ends."

Notable Students: Severus Snape, Phineas Nigellus, Merlin, Draco Malfoy, Tom Riddle

Values: Cunning, Ambition, Resourcefulness, Leadership

"Or yet in wise old Ravenclaw, if you've a ready mind, where those of wit and learning, will always find their kind."

Notable Students:Garrick Ollivander, Syvill Trelawney, Cho Chang, Luna Lovegood, Gilderoy Lockhart

Values: Intelligence, Wit, Wisdom, Creativity

"You might belong in Hufflepuff, where they are just and loyal, those patient Hufflepuffs are true, and unafraid of toil."

Notable Students: Cedric Diggory, Nymphadora Tonks, Pomona Sprout, Newt Scamander, Gabriel Truman

Values: Diligence, Loyalty, Kindness, Tolerance

"The Triwizard Tournament was first established some seven hundred years ago as a friendly competition between the three largest European schools of wizardry: Hogwarts, Beauxbatons, and Durmstrang. A champion was selected to represent each school, and the three champions competed in three magical tasks. The Ministry of Magic has decided that this year, the time is ripe for another attempt to revive this prestigious event. As a 7th year of house, and the brightest student of the year, you have been selected as the Hogwarts champion! Using the knowledge gained throughout your magical education, and the friendships you've built up over the years, bring eternal glory to your school, but be sure to live to tell the tale!
